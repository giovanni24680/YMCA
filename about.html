About Oulm

A sub-brand to make community arrival feel closer, calmer, and more current.

One YMCA carries strong recognition, but awareness does not convert into attendance. Oulm reframes the invitation through place, tone, and participation paths.

Primary insight

The hurdle is emotional.

People struggle to picture themselves arriving and belonging.

What Oulm changes

Place before profile.

Homebase, venue stories, host credibility, and soft RSVP structure reduce the demand to commit socially before feeling safe.

Longer arc

Community as proof.

Attendance becomes verified hours, host points, introductions, and signals that connect to wider life outcomes.
